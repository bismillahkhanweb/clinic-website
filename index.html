About Dr. Khan

Dedicated to your health with expertise, empathy, and evidence-based medicine.

A Physician You Can Trust

Dr. Ayesha Khan is a board-certified Family Medicine physician with over 12 years of clinical experience. She completed her MBBS from King Edward Medical University and her FCPS residency at Mayo Hospital, Lahore.

Her approach combines the latest medical evidence with genuine compassion. She believes every patient deserves time, attention, and a treatment plan tailored to their unique circumstances — not a one-size-fits-all solution.

Dr. Khan has served over 5,000 patients across her career, building long-term relationships that span generations of families. Her clinic is designed to feel welcoming, not clinical.
